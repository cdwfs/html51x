Accessibility

<label> and <fieldset> elements make forms significantly more accessible, for both disabled users and mobile browsers. Inputs can be separate or nested within their assocated labels, but the latter can make CSS styling easier. Also worth specifying the language of the label.

An input with a "required" attribute will trigger internal validation when the form is submitted. Common mistakes like out-of-range numbers, invalid dates or malformed email addresses will be detected automatically, and users will be prompted to correct them.
